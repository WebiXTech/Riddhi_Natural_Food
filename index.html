About Riddhi Natural Food

We are a family-owned business dedicated to bringing pure, traditional food ingredients from Kerala's finest Nendran bananas. Our powder is made with minimal processing to retain the natural resistant starch and aroma. Every batch is FSSAI certified and packed with care.

Wholesale & retail enquiries welcome – we ship across India. With over 26 years of experience in traditional processing, we ensure the highest quality standards for our customers.
